01 — How it works

From raw signal
to executed trade.

01

Data Acquisition

Raw market data is captured via our Scraper API at $0.0015 per record. Each snapshot includes price, volume, market cap, and 40+ technical fields. Yahoo Finance serves as a live fallback. A completeness score (0–100%) is assigned to every record before it advances.

02

Enrichment & Triage

Our null-triage system detects missing fields and routes to the cheapest provider that can fill them — Finnhub first, then FMP, then Alpha Vantage. Three providers run in parallel. The resulting record is deep-merged and validated before advancing to the AI nodes.

03

AI Intelligence Layer

Claude Sonnet 4 classifies the market regime every 15 minutes across 7 states. Simultaneously, the sentiment scorer processes the last 4 hours of news, analyst upgrades, and insider transactions — outputting a score from −1 to +1 with a black-swan detection flag.

04

Execution & Protection

Signals that clear the 72% confidence gate are routed to our RL Execution Node, which selects market, limit, or stop-limit order type based on real-time L2 order book depth. The Circuit Breaker monitors 9 kill conditions continuously, cancelling all open positions within 8ms of a trigger.
