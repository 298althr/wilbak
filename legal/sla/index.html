1. Institutional Uptime

Wilbak Engineering commits to a minimum of 99.99% operational uptime for all Ortho'M8 intelligence nodes. Our failure-resistant architecture is distributed across geographically independent data anchors to ensure zero downtime execution.

2. Performance Thresholds

  • Node Precision: 95% minimum confidence requirement per market instruction.
  • Execution Latency: Sub-320ms instruction transmission from DML gate to exchange node.
  • Risk Gate Speed: Hard-risk isolation parameters applied within 15ms of volatility detection.

3. Technical Support

Strategic partners gain access to a dedicated principal engineer. Response times for critical infrastructure inquiries are guaranteed within 4 hours. General system inquiries are addressed within 12 hours.

4. SLA Credits

In the event that the 99.99% uptime threshold is not met within an institutional cycle, Wilbak Engineering will apply tiered service credits toward upcoming audit cycles as outlined in your Partner Manifesto.
