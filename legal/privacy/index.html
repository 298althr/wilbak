1. Overview

Wilbak Engineering is committed to absolute security of your institutional and personal data. This protocol outlines how our deep machine learning nodes process data and how we safeguard your information.

2. Data Ingestion

Our algorithms ingest market data, order flow information, and partner asset metrics. This data is normalized and processed within encrypted, non-custodial environments. We do not store original raw data longer than necessary for node execution (typically 320ms).

3. Third-Party Sharing

Wilbak Engineering does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your identifiable information to outside parties. Your data is your capital anchor; we only interact with it to secure its growth within the Ortho'M8 ecosystem.

4. Security Architecture

We implement a variety of security measures including SSL encryption, multi-factor node verification, and regular penetration testing by independent security leads. Our uptime of 99.99% is matched by our zero-access data policy.

5. Partner Consent

By onboarding via our audit protocol, you consent to our data processing methodology. Any changes to this policy will be communicated directly through institutional Client Portal.
